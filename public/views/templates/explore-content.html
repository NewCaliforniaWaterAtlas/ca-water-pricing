Explore User Submitted Data

What users are saying about their water

Visitors to this site are invited to submit information from their water bills. With such a public dataset, we will be able to better rank the performance of our water utilities, and better understand whether our water services are on a sustainable path both financially and environmentally. Join us in this effort and contribute information from your water bill.

Explore Agency Data

What users reported to their water agencies

In collaboration with the California Public Utilities Commission, we plan to release data gathered from investor owned utilities statewide. These private utilities represent 20% of urban water providers in California. If you are a water agency that is part of the other 80%, and would like to participate in making your data available to the public, please reach out to us: info (at) statewater (dot) org
