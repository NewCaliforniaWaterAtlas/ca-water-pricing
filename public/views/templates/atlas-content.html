Introduction

The California Water Table data contains 30 years of underground water table level readings from scientific research wells across the state. Some data is collected by the state, other data is collected by individuals.

Data Stats

  • 56 counties
  • 18996 research wells measuring the top position (depth) of groundwater
  • 570,000+ measurements
  • 515 distinct groundwater systems
  • 431 groundwater basins
  • 24 basins are subdivided to create an additional 108 subbasins

Get Involved

  • add Spanish language support
  • code a web service to retrieve new agency data
